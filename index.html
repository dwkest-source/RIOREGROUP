The Process

Simple. Certain. Done.

We've removed every unnecessary step from the traditional sale process. Four steps. No uncertainty. No drawn-out negotiations.

01
Contact Us About Your Property
Reach out by phone or fill out our simple form with basic details about your property — location, condition, and your situation. There's absolutely no commitment required and everything is kept completely confidential.

What we need: Property address, your contact info, and a brief description of the property's condition. No paperwork, no agent meetings, no open houses.

02
Receive a Fair Cash Offer
We'll do a quick evaluation of your property and present you with a fair, no-obligation cash offer on the spot. No waiting weeks, no lowball numbers, no wasting your time.

What to expect: A real offer based on actual market data. We factor in location, condition, and comparable sales so you know exactly where we're coming from. No repairs needed before we evaluate.

03
Accept Our Offer & Choose Your Moving Date
Review the offer on your timeline. If it works for you, we open escrow immediately and handle all the paperwork. You choose your moving date — we work around your schedule.

No pressure: There is never any obligation to accept. We understand this is a significant decision and we respect that completely.

04
Close & Collect Your Cash
We close on your schedule — as fast as 14 days or whenever you're ready. Funds wired directly to you at close. No agent commissions, no repair credits, no last-minute renegotiations. It's that simple.

At closing: What we offer is what you receive — guaranteed. The number doesn't change between offer and close.
