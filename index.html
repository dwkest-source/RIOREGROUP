The Process

Simple. Certain. Done.

We've simplified the sale process to four straightforward steps. No uncertainty, no drawn-out negotiations — just a clear path from conversation to closing.

01
Tell Us About Your Property
Share basic details about the property — location, condition, and your situation. No commitment required at this stage.
02
Receive a Cash Offer
We'll evaluate the property and present a fair, no-obligation cash offer within 24–48 hours. No repairs needed, no showings.
03
Review & Accept
Review the offer on your timeline. If it works for you, we'll open escrow immediately and handle all the paperwork.
04
Close & Get Paid
We close on your schedule — as fast as 14 days or whenever you're ready. Funds wired directly at close.
